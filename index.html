About me

Hey! I'm Najm Ajmal, an enthusiastic developer with a strong passion for web development and programming. I love building projects, from tinkering with code to creating functional websites and services, and deploying them on platforms like GitHub and Vercel. My journey into computing was sparked by the Raspberry Pi, a small single-board computer that ignited my curiosity and love for technology.

I specialise in Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, and Markdown, and I use tools like Visual Studio Code, Git, GitHub, CodePen, Docker, Linux, and Replit to bring my ideas to life. I've contributed to opensource projects like pi-vpn and am always eager to explore new ways to solve problems through code.

Web Development is not just my job; it's my Passion.
